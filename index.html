About us

Hello and Welcome to 24k Production!

We are a Roblox Production Team that tries their best to:

•Develop games for all ages

•Develop games at the fastest way possible

24k Production only has 1 goal, which is to bring joyfulness to the ROBLOX community. Since the group does not care about payments, our games aren’t pay to win! Even though we do not care about payments, we will most definitely appreciate any donations and support, even if it is as small as 5 robux!

We are currently working on many projects, and they’re all different in their own way! Some might be simple, while others could be the best experiences you play in your entire life. All we ask of you, is to be mindful, appreciative, and respectful to all our staff and community. Every single one of them has a great role in our success, and we can not bring them down.

We provide the highest quality games with AAA game developers who have extensive amount of experience and time developing and playing on the roblox platform.
- Dodger, Senior Lead Developer at 24k Production.


Some of our brand virtues are:
Quality over quantity
Gameplay over money
Respect for the community
Always listining to the community
Always open to new ideas
Community based projects, not money based projects
